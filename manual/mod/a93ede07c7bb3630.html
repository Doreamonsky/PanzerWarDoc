    36. Upload to Official ModHub 上传模组到官方平台

    We understand that creating mods is not easy, but due to reasons such as model accuracy and data parameters, you may encounter dissatisfaction from some players. Please ensure you are mentally prepared before proceeding with the upload.

    我们明白制作模组不易，但由于模型考据、数据参数等原因，您可能会遭到部分玩家的不满。请确保您已经做好充分的心理准备再进行上传。

    Upload Qualifications 上传资格

    All mod authors can upload their mods without undergoing a review process. However, only mods that are original are eligible for upload. Please ensure that the mods you upload comply with the CC BY-NC license. By uploading a mod, you agree to the principles of free and open sharing, making the mod freely available for distribution by any player without restrictions. Note: Mods with copyright disputes will not qualify for incentives.

    所有模组作者可以无需审核直接上传模组。但请确保上传的模组是自己制作的。上传的模组必须遵守 CC BY-NC 协议。上传即代表自愿公开流通模组，同意模组以自由的传播方式，不限制任何玩家进行传播。注意：上传的模组若存在版权争议将无法获得激励金。

    Tool Usages 工具用法

    Open the tool - 打开工具

    Enter Your Account - 输入你的账号密码

    Configure your account and click save at the bottom

    配置你的账号，然后点击底部的保存选项

    Pick the account by clicking this button

    点击这个标记的按钮，选择你的账号

    Pick File 选择文件

    Pick a modpack file to upload，or pick a folder by the second button

    选择模组文件进行上传，或者通过第二个按钮，批量上传文件夹中的 modpack 文件

    If you need to upgrade the mod, then need to change the version. +1 version to make it upgradable.

    若模组需要进行更新，则需要进行版本变更，需要 +1 的版本号，才可正常进行更新。

    Contract with us 与我们签订创作激励收益

    Once your original, high-quality, and free mod achieves a certain number of downloads on the mod downloader, we will offer you an incentive agreement. This agreement will provide a reward ranging from 50 to 500 SGD, depending on the quality of your mod. Please note that a Singapore bank account is required to receive the reward.

    一旦您的原创、高质量、免费的模组在模组下载器上达到一定的下载量，我们将与您签订激励协议。根据模组的质量，我们会提供 50 至 500 新币不等的奖励。请注意，需要提供一张新加坡银行卡来接收奖励。

    Prohibition of Illegal Use of Mods 禁止非法利用模组

    It is strictly forbidden for any user to utilize uploaded public mods and their related files for illegal fundraising, fraud, or commercial activities in any form. If such behavior is discovered, we will pursue legal action in accordance with the law.

    严禁任何使用者以任何形式利用已上传的公开模组及其相关文件进行非法集资、诈骗或商业活动。如发现此类行为，我们将依法追究相关法律责任。
